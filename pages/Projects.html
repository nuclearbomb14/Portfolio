Robotics

This project involved assembling a VEX V5 robotic rover with a claw being attached later on, alongside the help of a partner. This involved tools such as screwdrivers, motors, gears, and wiring. Once assembled, the rover required programming for functionality. The rover contains four programmable motors, with two controlling its movement, and two used to control the claw and lift.
